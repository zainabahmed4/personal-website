Gradient Design: Dawn & Dusk
Adobe Illustrator

In this graphic design project, I explored the contrasting themes of "Dawn" and "Dusk" by creating two gradient designs in Adobe Illustrator. Each gradient was crafted to evoke the distinct emotions, feelings, and colors associated with these times of day. The "Dawn" design features soft, uplifting hues to represent the calm and renewal of morning, while the "Dusk" design incorporates deeper, more muted tones to convey the serenity and transition of evening.

Password Checker
C++

Designed and implemented a C++ program to validate password strength based on criteria like capitalization, digits, length, and dictionary word inclusion. Enhanced security by cross-referencing passwords against a leaked-passwords database, utilizing efficient algorithms, binary search, and vectors to ensure robust validation and security.
